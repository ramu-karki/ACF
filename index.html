Who We Are

A Foundation Built on Compassion

Established in 2014, ACF is Nepal's dedicated cancer support organization — registered under the Organization Administration Act of 1978.

Astha Cancer Foundation (ACF), Nepal is a non-government, not-for-profit social organization founded in 2014 A.D. ACF is registered in the district administration office in Kathmandu, Nepal, committed to fighting cancer through education and community engagement.

We focus on cancer prevention and early detection by partnering with schools, colleges, and social groups to encourage healthier lifestyle decisions that can reduce cancer risk across Nepal. We also encourage exchange programs with various academic institutions around the world.

Purpose

Our Vision & Mission

Guided by a clear sense of purpose — a cancer-aware Nepal where every patient receives timely support and informed care.

Vision
A Society Free of Cancer's Shadow

To enlighten patients and community about early detection and prevention measures of cancer — so that early definitive treatment becomes the norm.

Mission
Knowledge as the Cure

To educate cancer patients, their families, and the population as a whole about cancer — its early signs, preventive measures, and pathways to effective treatment.

Goals

Key Objectives

Concrete, actionable goals that drive every program and initiative at the Astha Cancer Foundation.

01

Conduct informal cancer education and awareness programs across diverse communities in Nepal.

02

Provide free counseling services to cancer patients and their family members.

03

Offer free consultation sessions with qualified oncologists.

04

Conduct regular cancer awareness programs in schools.

05

Encourage international exchange programs for world‑class cancer management.

06

Provide financial support and medicines to helpless and needy cancer patients.
